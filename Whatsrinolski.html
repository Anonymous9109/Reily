What's Rinolski?

Rinolski's Goal

The goal of Rinolski is to give internet users the opportunity to enjoy movies and series without ads, microtransactions, or payments. It's completely free.

Are We Planning to Make It a Paid Service?

We have no funding, Rinolski started with $0 and is still running with $0. If our group grows and we need to pay employees or cover server costs, we'll look for ways to fund it. But no, we do not plan to make it a paid service any time soon.
